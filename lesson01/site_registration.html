Site Registration

You will register or update an existing registration with Br. Robertson's web site. Your registration will include the following:

  • Your name
  • A BYU-Idaho email address
  • A password
  • The class your are currently registered for: CIT 336
    • What section of the class:
    • 2:00 class is Section 01
    • 3:15 class is Section 05
  • The current semester: Spring 2016
  • The URL of your web site. Registration should occur after you have purchased your hosting and domain name.

Ensure It Worked

Once registered, check that it worked correctly by going to the Student Web Site List page and looking for your name. Click on your name and you should be taken to your web site.

If your name does not appear or the link does not work, return to your profile and double check your information. Continue to work with the registration until your name appears on the list page and the link works.
