Getting Help

For this class there are multiple avenues of help, but keep in mind that you must have tried to solve the problem yourself prior to seeking help from others.

Your Peers - it is expected and encouraged for you to work together to help one another. Meet face-to-face, call one another, use virtual software (Skype, Google Hangouts, etc...).

The Discussion Boards - use the weekly discussion boards to post and answer questions related to the topic of the week.

The Professor - Check the Instructor Profile page for information on when and how to talk with the professor.
