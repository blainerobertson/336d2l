Teaching Presentation Grading Matrix

Overview

The teaching presentation receives a score for two course objectives: 1 and 6.

Objective 1 - 10 points

  • 1 point - The teaching presentation will be posted to your web site by the scheduled date of the presentation and a link to the presentation placed in the footer of your site.
  • 3 points - The presentation document is semantically sound.
  • 3 points - The presentation document validates to HTML5 standards.
  • 3 points - The presentation document validates to CSS3 standards.

Objective 6 - 35 points

  • 5 points - The written materials in the presentation are grammatically correct and error free.
  • 5 points - Verbal communication is clear and focused on the topic being presented.
  • 5 points - The physical or video presentation is concise, focused and paced to fit within the allowed time of 7 minutes.
  • 10 points - The teaching presentation must provide accurate, thorough information about the topic so that the audience can learn about the topic from your materials.
  • 10 points - The teaching presentation must contain a working example with an accompanying explanation such that the example can be understood enough to be reproduced and used by the audience.
