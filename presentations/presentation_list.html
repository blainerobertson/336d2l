Teaching Presentation Topics

Directions

The teaching presentations listed below are organized by week. Claim a topic by going to the sign-up page (in this module) and signing up for a topic. Selection is done on a first-come first-serve basis by editing the document and adding your own name to the right of the topic in the column for your section.

Be sure to record your topic and date into a calendar with a reminder in order to be prepared and post your presentation page to your web site on time.

Submission and Due Date

Campus Sections

Your teaching presentation will be submitted by posting the presentation page (with working example(s)) to your website and placing a link to the presentation in your exercises page by the time of your in-class presentation.

Online Sections

Your teaching presentation will be submitted by posting the presentation page (with working example(s)) to your website and placing a link to the presentation in your exercises page by Tuesday @ 11pm. After posting your presentation you should post a notification to the weekly collaboration board (along with a link to your presentation) so others in the class can view your presentation.
