Teaching Presentation Topics

Directions

The teaching presentations listed below are organized by week. Claim a topic by going to the sign-up page (in this module) and signing up for a topic. Selection is done on a first-come first-serve basis by editing the document and adding your own name to the right of the topic in the column for your section.

Be sure to record your topic and date into a calendar with a reminder in order to be prepared and post your presentation page to your web site on time.

Submission

Campus and Online Sections

Objective 1 requires you to produce a web page and post it to your web site. The web page must contain information and a working example related to your presentation topic. Refer to the grading matrix for details related to objective 1.

Campus Sections

In addition to the posted web page you will present your topic in class on the scheduled date. The presentation must be under 7 minutes. You may use your web page or another presentation tool (PowerPoint, KeyNote, Prezzi, etc...) to present your topic. Refer to objective 6 for details about the presentation requirements. You must add a link to the weekly collaboration board pointing to your posted presenation web page by the time of your in-class presentation.

Online Sections

In addition to the posted web page you will create a video of you presenting your topic. The presentation video must be under 7 minutes. You may use your web page or another presentation tool (PowerPoint, KeyNote, Prezzi, etc...) to present your topic in the video. Refer to objective 6 for details about the presentation requirements. You must add a link to the weekly collaboration board pointing to your posted presenation web page AND to your presentation video by 11pm on Tuesday of the week of your scheduled presentation.
