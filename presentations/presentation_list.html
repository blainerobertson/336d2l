Teaching Presentation Topics

Directions

The teaching presentations listed below is organized by lesson. Claim a topic by going to the WIKI page and signing up for the topic. Selection is done on a first-come first-serve basis by editing the document and adding your own name to the right of the topic for your section. The first column is for section 1 (2pm class) while the right column is for section 2 (3:15pm class).

Be sure to record your topic and date into a calendar with a reminder in order to be prepared and post your video and other presentation materials on time.

Submission and Due Date

Your teaching presentation will be submitted by posting the video and working example page(s) to your website and placing a link to the presentation in your site footer by Wednesday of the lesson it is due. Once posted you will add a post to the lesson discussion board with a URL to the presentation so class peers can view and comment on your presentation.
