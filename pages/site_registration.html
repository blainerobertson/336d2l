Site Registration

In order for others in the class and the Professor / Teaching Assistant to find your work we need to know where your web site is located. You will add your name and site URL to the Student Site List to make this happen. [Note: You must have your domain name and site hosting before doing this!]

Register Your Site

Go to the Site List Sign-up Directions page (in the Student Web Sites module) and follow the steps listed there to register your site for the class.

Ensure It Worked

Once registered, check that it worked correctly by clicking on your name and confirming that your site opens in a new tab.

The Student Site List

Later, to visit your site from the class list, go to the Student Web Sites module and click the "Student Web Site List" link.
