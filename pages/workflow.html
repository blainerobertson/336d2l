Development Workflow

A general confusion seems to be expressed in this question, "How do I develop and test my site locally and then get it out for the world to see?" The answer is to follow a work flow. In our situation we have a local server (XAMPP), an IDE (NetBeans), an FTP client (FileZilla) and a production server (your web hosting account). How do all of these work together to get the job done?

The video below demonstrates how this happens. Please watch it carefully and perhaps even follow along to make sure it works for you too.
